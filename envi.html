ENVIRONMENT

  • The focus of environmental initiatives to bring about a positive change among individuals and
    communities in the way they treat their environment by promoting eco-friendly practices and through awareness campaigns.
  • Waste Segregation:
    Enable community to segregate waste at source, and train them on best practices like composting and reduced use of plastic.
  • Sapling Plantation:
    Plant and nurture saplings to ensure maximum survival rate.
  • Swacch Bharat:
    Encourage and faciliate people’s participation in cleanliness of surroundings.
    Bring about an improvement in the general quality of life by promoting cleanliness, hygiene and eliminating open defecation.
