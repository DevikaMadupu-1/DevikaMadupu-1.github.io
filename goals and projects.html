GOALS AND PROJECTS

GOALS

  • The goal of YFS is to support schools, destitute shelters, government hospitals and other organizations in the social sector through volunteers who can help them mitigate some of their shortcomings and challenges.
  • Volunteering is made easy and customized to the individual’s interests, time availability and skill sets.
  • YFS aims to create positive change agents in the society, through the culture of volunteering.
